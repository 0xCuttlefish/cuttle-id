The GDRC 1 is a visual interpretation of the GDRC Charter made by artist @6529er.

It can be minted on the Ethereum blockchain. It is free to mint, except for your wallet's gas/transaction fee and has no royalties associated with it.

The art has been placed in the public domain using the Creative Commons 0 (CC0) license by @6529er.

More details can be found at the FAQ page.
